Eucharistic Miracles

When the Invisible is Made Visible

Eucharistic miracles are extraordinary signs that the Church believes God occasionally permits to reveal in an observable way that He truly is present in the Eucharist. In most of these events, it is not only the substance that changes — as Catholics believe happens at every Mass — but the appearance as well: ordinary bread and wine become, visibly, flesh and blood.
