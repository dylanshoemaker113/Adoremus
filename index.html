The Source and Summit of the Christian Life

“This is My Body.”

Luke 22:19

Catholics believe that in the Eucharist, Jesus Christ is truly present — Body, Blood, Soul, and Divinity. This belief has shaped Christian worship for two thousand years and continues to transform lives today. Whether you have believed this your whole life, or you have never heard it before, you are welcome here.

Welcome

A Living Mystery, Hidden in Plain Sight

Every day, in tens of thousands of churches around the world, ordinary bread and wine are consecrated and become the actual Body and Blood of Jesus Christ, while still looking, tasting, and feeling like bread and wine. This site exists to explain that belief: where it comes from, why the Church has always taught it, and what it means for you, whether you are a lifelong Catholic, a curious skeptic, or someone simply looking for Jesus.

A Living Tradition

When Heaven Confirms What Faith Already Knows

Throughout history, the Church has investigated extraordinary events in which the appearance of the Eucharist itself has changed — hosts that became visible flesh and blood, consecrated hosts that survived fires, floods, and decay. These are not the foundation of Catholic belief in the Real Presence — Christ's own words are — but they are striking confirmations of it.
